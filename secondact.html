Horster

I thought you might all sit here; you can slip out easily from here, if things get too lively.

Mrs Stockmann

Do you think there will be a disturbance?

Horster

One can never tell—with such a crowd. But sit down, and don’t be uneasy.

Mrs Stockmann

(sitting down). It was extremely kind of you to offer my husband the room.

Horster

Well, if nobody else would

Petra

(who has sat down beside her mother). And it was a plucky thing to do, Captain Horster.

Horster

Oh, it is not such a great matter as all that.(HOVSTAD and ASLAKSEN make their way through the crowd.)
